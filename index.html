Getting Started

Initial Setup

Welcome to your barbershop booking system! Follow these steps to get your business up and running:

  1. Access Your Dashboard: Navigate to your Shopify admin panel and find the Barbershop Pro app
  2. Complete Initial Configuration: Set up your shop information, locations, and basic settings
  3. Add Your Staff: Create staff profiles with photos, contact information, and specialties
  4. Configure Services: Set up your service offerings with pricing and duration
  5. Set Availability: Define when your staff are available for appointments

Dashboard Overview

Your main dashboard provides a comprehensive view of your business:

  • Today's Appointments: See all scheduled appointments for the current day
  • Staff Performance: Track bookings and customer satisfaction
  • Revenue Analytics: Monitor your business performance
  • Quick Actions: Fast access to common tasks

Staff Management

Adding New Staff Members

  1. Navigate to Staff ‚Üí Add New Staff
  2. Fill in the required information:
    • Full name (required)
    • Email address
    • Phone number
    • Bio and specialties
    • Profile photo
  3. Set their active status (active staff can receive bookings)
  4. Click Create Staff Member

Managing Staff Profiles

From the staff list, you can:

  • View Details: Click on any staff member's name to access their profile
  • Edit Information: Update contact details, bio, or photo
  • Set Availability: Configure when each staff member is available
  • Manage Services: Assign which services each staff member can perform
  • Deactivate Staff: Temporarily disable staff without deleting their data

Staff Availability

Configure when your staff members are available to work:

  • Weekly Schedules: Set recurring availability patterns (e.g., Monday-Friday, 9 AM-5 PM)
  • Date-Specific Availability: Override weekly schedules for holidays or special events
  • Time Slots: Define specific time intervals for appointments
  • Location-Based: If you have multiple locations, assign staff to specific venues

Booking System

Customer Booking Process

Your customers can book appointments through your integrated booking widget:

  1. Select Service: Choose from your available services (haircut, beard trim, etc.)
  2. Pick Date & Time: View real-time availability and select preferred slot
  3. Choose Staff: Select their preferred barber (optional)
  4. Enter Details: Provide contact information and any special requests
  5. Confirm Booking: Review and confirm their appointment

Managing Appointments

From your dashboard, you can:

  • View All Bookings: See appointments by date, staff, or status
  • Edit Appointments: Reschedule or modify existing bookings
  • Mark as Arrived: Check in customers when they arrive
  • Cancel Appointments: Handle cancellations with automatic notifications
  • Add Notes: Include special instructions or customer preferences

Automated Notifications

The system automatically sends:

  • Booking Confirmations: Immediate confirmation emails
  • Reminder Notifications: 24-hour and 2-hour appointment reminders
  • Cancellation Alerts: Notifications when appointments are cancelled
  • Rescheduling Updates: Alerts when appointments are modified

Shopify Integration

Product Synchronization

Your barbershop services are automatically synced with your Shopify store:

  • Service Products: Each booking service becomes a Shopify product
  • Inventory Management: Track service availability and capacity
  • Pricing Sync: Service prices automatically update in Shopify
  • Product Variants: Different service options (e.g., haircut vs. haircut + beard)

Order Processing

When customers book appointments:

  1. An order is automatically created in Shopify
  2. Payment is processed through your existing Shopify payment gateway
  3. Customer information is synced to your Shopify customer database
  4. Appointment details are linked to the order for easy tracking

Multi-Location Support

If you have multiple barbershop locations:

  • Location-Specific Services: Different services at different locations
  • Staff Assignment: Assign staff to specific locations
  • Separate Inventories: Manage capacity for each location independently
  • Unified Dashboard: View all locations from one central interface

Availability Management

Setting Up Weekly Availability

  1. Go to Staff ‚Üí [Staff Member] ‚Üí Weekly Availability
  2. Click Add New Schedule
  3. Configure:
    • Days of the week (Monday, Tuesday, etc.)
    • Start and end times
    • Available/Unavailable status
    • Location (if multiple locations)
  4. Save the schedule

Managing Date-Specific Availability

For holidays, vacations, or special events:

  1. Navigate to Staff ‚Üí [Staff Member] ‚Üí Date Availability
  2. Click Add New Date Schedule
  3. Select the specific date
  4. Set custom hours or mark as unavailable
  5. Add notes for special instructions

Time Slot Configuration

Control how appointments are scheduled:

  • Interval Duration: Set how long each appointment slot lasts (15, 30, 60 minutes)
  • Buffer Time: Add time between appointments for cleanup
  • Advance Booking: Limit how far in advance customers can book
  • Same-Day Booking: Allow or restrict same-day appointments

Troubleshooting

Common Issues & Solutions

‚ùå Staff members not showing in booking widget

Solution: Ensure staff members are marked as "Active" in their profile. Inactive staff won't appear in the booking system.

‚ùå No available time slots showing

Solution: Check that staff have availability schedules set up. Go to Staff ‚Üí [Staff Member] ‚Üí Weekly Availability and ensure schedules are configured.

‚ùå Shopify products not syncing

Solution: Verify your Shopify integration is active. Check that services have proper variants and pricing configured.

‚ùå Customers not receiving confirmation emails

Solution: Check your email settings in the Shopify admin. Ensure your store's email configuration is properly set up.

‚ùå Booking widget not loading

Solution: Clear your browser cache and refresh the page. If the issue persists, check that the app is properly installed and activated.
